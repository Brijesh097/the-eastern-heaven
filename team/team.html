Our Team @ The Eastern Heaven

We are small but mighty. The Eastern Heaven has been a family owned and run business since the dirty thirties, and we're proud of it! When you get these three together, you never know what can happen. But you can count on one thing: The best food you've ever had. Ever.

Miles Heizer

Miles is the great-grandson of Brandon Heizer. He is the owner of The Eastern Heaven Restaurant. He cooks a mean Fritatta!

Katherine Langford

Katherine knows her stuff. The big sister of Miles himself, she runs the show. Don't miss her Margherita Mondays!

Brandon Flynn

Brandon is the epitome of the phrase "Don't judge a book by its cover" - you simply cannot find a better chef other than him.
